‚ùì FAQ

Why does first load take so long?
The AI model (~2GB) downloads once and is cached. After that, it loads in seconds.
Is my data sent anywhere?
No. Everything runs 100% locally using WebGPU. Zero data leaves your browser.
Why is analysis slow?
Each video is analyzed individually (~1-2s each). GPU-enabled devices are faster.
WebGPU not supported?
Use Chrome 113+ or Edge 113+. Check chrome://gpu for WebGPU status.
